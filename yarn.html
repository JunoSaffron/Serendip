Yarn Sourcing

Serendip Sourcing takes pride in sourcing high quality yarns which are natural, organic, regular and sustainable. Yarn plays a major role in every fabric. The strength, texture, shade and count of the chosen yarn contributes to the quality, look and knitting style. The right yarn chosen enhances production by reducing wastage and time consumption.


We source yarn, labels and accessories from UK, India, Germany, turkey, Italy, Ireland, France and Spain. We work with 2/28, 2/30, 2/34, 2/20, 3/30, 1/7, 1/10.5, 1/8, 1/15, 1/18 and far other counts. Our yarns are in stock around the year to shorten our lead times and carter delivery in time.


Our excellent goodwill and relevant contact with major yarn producers from many countries have strengthened our network and procurement abilities. All natural, synthetic and recycled yarns can be sourced in accordance with the quality and quantity required.
