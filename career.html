Employee Welfare

India is lagging behind in public health care system, we are providing private medical insurance to all our employees to ensure we stand with them during their difficult times.

Commuting to labourers is provided free of cost. In future, we are going to use electric vans to commute them to work.

In India, buying a home is very difficult for many labourers in their life-time. Due to lack of education, they are unable to approach and avail banking services. We help our employees with arranging home loans at the lowest available interest rate. First home loan has been given to our labourer.

In India, people lend money from money lenders at unreasonable interest rates due to which they are indebted forever. We arrange personal loans at reasonable interest rates to pay back those debts at a faster pace..

As a company, we are committed to ensure health, housing and financial safety to all our employees.
