about us

We are a Knitwear manufacturer based in India. We make the styles for adults, kids and for the newborn using 100% organic cotton, 100% wool, cotton blended recycled polyester, mulesing- free merino wool and lamb’s wool.

* Based on the customer request we do acrylic and blended acrylic styles for kids and adult style.

  • Packing
  • Warehouse
